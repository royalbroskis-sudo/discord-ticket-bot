Setup

Get set up in three steps

No coding, no config files — everything happens in the browser.

1

Add the bot

Click "Add to Discord" above and choose the server you want to set it up in. You'll need the "Manage Server" permission.

2

Log in with Discord

Sign in to the dashboard with your Discord account to see every server you manage.

3

Turn on what you need

Pick a server, flip on the features you want — autorole, tickets, AutoMod, and more — and save.

Ready to set up your server?

It takes less than two minutes to get the bot in and your first feature configured.
